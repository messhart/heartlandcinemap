Heartland Cinemap

About

Are you the kind of person living in the midwest US who would do ANYTHING to catch a screening you want to see, at a cinema you love — but struggle to find where they are in the middle of corn (and probably soybean) fields? This site is built for you.

Heartland Cinemap lists the showtimes at independent and non-profit cinemas around the Midwest, existing to connect people to the beautiful images and sounds of our times. Browse what's screening near you (in midwest standards), build a film calendar of your own, and enjoy the movies. Please note that showtimes and ticket availability may change.

Including venues in states, list growing.
